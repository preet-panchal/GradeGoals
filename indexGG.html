WELCOME TO GRADE GOALS!

This is a simple grade calculator that allows you to set your personal grade goal in a course and calculates how much you will need for each assessment to achieve your grade goal in the course.

Just 4 Simple Steps:

  1. Enter course name.
  2. Enter the number of assessments in the course.
  3. Enter grade goal in the course (out of 100%).
  4. Fill out the table with table below as best as possible.
  5. Click Calculate!
